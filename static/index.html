      About CassavaGuard

      An AI-powered diagnostic platform for cassava leaf disease classification. Built on EfficientNet-B4 transfer learning to assist agricultural researchers and field workers in identifying crop diseases rapidly and accurately.

      EfficientNet-B4 Backbone

      Transfer learning with EfficientNet-B4 pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned for robust 5-class cassava pathology classification with 1792-dim feature extraction.

      Two-Phase Training

      Phase 1: frozen backbone, train FC head. Phase 2: fine-tune last residual block with cosine annealing.

      Actionable Insights

      Beyond classification — disease descriptions, symptom identification, and recommended agricultural interventions.
